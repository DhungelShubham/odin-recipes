Mounton-Chicken-Momo

This is a finished photo of Mounton-Chicken-Momo you can make at home. using the following recipe .This recipe is from the website allrecipes.

INGREDIENTS:

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • kima
  • Cheeses
  • flour

STEPS:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the momos
  3. Make the mixture of kima and flour .
  4. Layer the momo according to the recipe instructions.
  5. steam them.
  6. Let the momoa rest before serving.
