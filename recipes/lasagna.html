Lasagna

This is a finished photo of lasagna you can make at home. using the following recipe .This recipe is from the website allrecipes.

INGREDIENTS:

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

STEPS:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
